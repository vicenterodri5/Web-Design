Change in American Beverage Consumption
Predicted for 2016

Growth of Energy, Water and Sports Drinks

  • Pepsi and other major beverage companies are all about developing new healthy beverages -- or at least brands perceived as healthier -- in the new year. Pepsi is launching products like --
    • organic Gatorade
    • new Aquafina flavored waters
    • healthy vending machines

Reworking Recipes

  • Core brands like Coke and Pepsi need to be reformulated with fewer calories or replaced with revamped versions
  • Examples:
    • Mountain Dew Kickstart
    • Pepsi removed aspartame from Diet Pepsi
    • Coke Life

Smaller Cans and Bottles

2014
the American Beverage Association pledged to cut calories by 20% by 2025. By serving smaller cans and bottles, can do just that, without necessarily cutting sugar from the recipe.

Attempts at Authenticity

  • Tapping into shoppers' nostalgia (vintage inspired drinks)
  • Earn external symbols of approval.
  • Craft Beverages
